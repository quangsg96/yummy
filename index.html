CELEBRATE JUNETEENTH: A Lesson in Hair-Story

In celebration of Juneteenth, Yummy Hair Extensions wants to pay homage to the innovation in hair styling that those who came before us so boldly ventured into so that we all could boldly express ourselves through our hairstyles today. Abraham Lincoln declared the enslaved African American population free on January 1st 1863. However, the last of the enslaved African American population who were located in Texas were not freed until June 19th 1865.
